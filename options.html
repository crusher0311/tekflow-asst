Instructions

1. Enter the customer's concern in the input box and click "Submit Concern".

2. Follow-up questions will be listed in the Follow-Up List section. Click on a question to move it to the active area.

3. Enter the customer's response to each follow-up question and click "Submit".

4. Use the "Save" button to save the conversation when complete.
